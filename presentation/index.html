Before: 12-Step Flow

  • Login (CAPTCHA + OTP)
  • Navigate to "Plan My Journey"
  • Enter source (7000+ stations)
  • Enter destination
  • Select date
  • Choose class
  • Click "Find Trains"
  • Scroll 50+ results
  • Select train + quota
  • Enter passenger details (manual)
  • Enter CAPTCHA (again)
  • Payment (3 redirects)

After: 5-Step Flow

  • Login (OTP only, trusted devices)
  • Search (typeahead, saved routes)
  • Select Train (top 10, sorted)
  • Passenger Details (saved + quick add)
  • Payment (in-page, UPI first)
